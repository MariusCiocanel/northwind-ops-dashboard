# Northwind Coffee Co. — team & operating context

## The company

Northwind Coffee Co. is a **young, fast-growing** direct-to-consumer specialty coffee roaster. We're still small enough that the team runs the business out of spreadsheets — which is exactly the problem we're here to fix. We sell whole-bean and ground coffee, cold brew, and a bit of merch, through two channels:

- **Northwind web store** (our own site) — the majority of revenue, and where our **subscription** program lives.
- **Amazon** — one-time orders, lower margin, more price-sensitive.

We roast to order on a weekly cycle, so inventory and demand have to stay in sync — stockouts on a fast-moving roast cost us real revenue, and over-roasting means stale beans we have to discount or write off.

## What they need from the dashboard

- One view of **sales** and one of **inventory** — switchable, not two separate tools.
- The ability to **filter and sort by SKU, roast, size, and channel**, and to drill from a summary number down into the detail behind it.
- The **priority metrics from the weekly sync front and center** — they don't want to hunt for them.
- Honest numbers. If a roast is sliding or about to stock out, they want to see it immediately, not discover it next week.

## Product line (for reference)

Coffee comes in **12oz** and **2lb** bags. Roasts: **Sunrise** (light), **House** (medium), **Midnight** (dark), **Espresso**, plus single origins **Ethiopia** and **Colombia**, and a **Decaf**. Non-coffee: **Cold Brew Concentrate**, a **Sampler** pack, and two merch items (**Mug**, **Tumbler**). SKU codes and current prices live in the spreadsheets.
